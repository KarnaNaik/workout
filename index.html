Part 3: Exercise Library

1. Push-ups

Muscles: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps
How to Perform:
  1. Start in a high plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width.
  2. Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels. Core tight.
  3. Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor.
  4. Push back up explosively to the starting position.
Tip: Don't flare elbows. Keep them tucked at 45 degrees.
Regression: Perform on knees if needed.

2. Squats

Muscles: Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings
How to Perform:
  1. Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointing slightly outward.
  2. Push hips back as if sitting in a chair.
  3. Lower until thighs are parallel to the floor (or lower).
  4. Drive through heels to return to standing.
Tip: Keep chest up. Do not let knees collapse inward.

3. Leg Raises

Muscles: Lower Abs, Hip Flexors
How to Perform:
  1. Lie flat on your back, legs straight, hands under glutes for support.
  2. Lift legs toward the ceiling until hips slightly leave the floor.
  3. Slowly lower legs back down without letting heels touch the ground.
Tip: Press lower back into the floor to protect your spine.

4. Mountain Climbers

Muscles: Core, Shoulders, Cardio
How to Perform:
  1. Start in a high plank position.
  2. Drive one knee toward your chest, keeping the other leg extended.
  3. Quickly switch legs, driving the opposite knee forward.
  4. Continue alternating at a fast pace.
Tip: Keep hips level; avoid bouncing your butt up and down.

5. Hollow Hold

Muscles: Deep Core (Transverse Abdominis)
How to Perform:
  1. Lie on your back with arms extended overhead and legs straight.
  2. Lift shoulders and legs off the ground simultaneously.
  3. Hold this position. Only your lower back should touch the floor.
Tip: If lower back arches, lift legs higher to correct it.

6. Plank

Muscles: Entire Core
How to Perform:
  1. Place forearms on the floor, elbows directly under shoulders.
  2. Extend legs back, resting on toes.
  3. Maintain a straight line from head to heels. Squeeze glutes.

7. Wall Push-ups

Muscles: Chest, Shoulders (Beginner Friendly)
How to Perform:
  1. Stand arm's length away from a wall.
  2. Place palms on the wall at shoulder height.
  3. Bend elbows to lower your chest toward the wall.
  4. Push back to the start.

8. Shoulder Taps

Muscles: Core Stability
How to Perform:
  1. Start in a high plank position.
  2. Lift one hand and tap the opposite shoulder while keeping hips still.
  3. Return hand to floor and switch sides.

9. Lunges

Muscles: Quads, Glutes
How to Perform:
  1. Stand tall. Step one foot forward.
  2. Lower hips until both knees are bent at 90 degrees.
  3. Push off the front foot to return to standing. Alternate legs.

10. Glute Bridges

Muscles: Glutes, Hamstrings
How to Perform:
  1. Lie on back, knees bent, feet flat on the floor.
  2. Drive through heels to lift hips toward ceiling.
  3. Squeeze glutes hard at the top. Lower slowly.

Forearm Specialization

11. Wrist Extension

Muscles: Outer Forearm
How to Perform:
  1. Stand with arms relaxed at your sides.
  2. Extend your wrists backward (lifting palms upward) as far as possible.
  3. Return to neutral. Perform rhythmically.

12. Standing Wrist Flexion

Muscles: Inner Forearm
How to Perform:
  1. Raise hands to chest height, elbows bent (boxing guard).
  2. Clench fists.
  3. Flex wrists inward and downward forcefully. Release and repeat.

13. Wrist Waves

Muscles: Mobility
How to Perform:
  1. Hold hands in front of chest, palms facing each other.
  2. Create a fluid, wave-like motion rolling from wrists through fingers.
  3. Keep movement continuous.

14. Wrist Rotations

Muscles: Wrist Health
How to Perform:
  1. Clench fists in front of you.
  2. Rotate your wrists in circular motions.
  3. Ensure full range of motion.

Part 4: Yoga Library

15. Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Focus: Spine Elongation, Posture
How to Perform:
  1. Stand with feet together. Interlock fingers and turn palms upward.
  2. Inhale and stretch arms up, lifting heels off the floor.
  3. Balance on toes and stretch your whole body upward.
  4. Hold while breathing deeply. Exhale and lower heels.
Benefit: Decompresses spinal discs and corrects posture.

16. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Focus: Testosterone, Spine Flexibility
How to Perform:
  1. Lie on your stomach, legs together, hands under shoulders.
  2. Inhale and lift your chest off the floor, keeping elbows slightly bent.
  3. Look upward and hold. Keep pubic bone on the floor.
Benefit: Reduces cortisol (stress) to aid testosterone production.

17. Sarvangasana (Shoulder Stand)

Focus: HGH Release, Thyroid Health
How to Perform:
  1. Lie on back. Lift legs and hips toward the ceiling.
  2. Support your lower back with your hands.
  3. Straighten legs so body forms a vertical line from shoulders to toes.
  4. Chin should press against chest (chin lock).
Benefit: Stimulates the thyroid gland which regulates metabolism and growth hormones.

18. Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend)

Focus: Hamstrings, Spine Stretch
How to Perform:
  1. Sit with legs straight in front of you.
  2. Inhale and lengthen spine. Exhale and hinge at hips to reach for feet.
  3. Keep knees straight. Relax head toward knees.
